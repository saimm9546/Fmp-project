All in One Computer

Acer, Apple, ASUS, Dell, HP, Lenovo

All-in-One (AIO) PCs are personal computers that have incorporated many components of a traditional desktop computer into a single, compact unit.Traditional desktop computers have a monitor and a separate computer case that holds the motherboard, hard drive, and all other components. AIO PCs differ, storing all components, bar the keyboard and mouse, into the monitor.Many AIO PCs feature integrated touch screen monitors, touch-pads, and solid-state disk drives and memory. They tend to have a smaller profile than other desktop computers, reduce clutter, and come with sleek designs.
